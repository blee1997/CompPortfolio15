Mouse, Keyboard, and Monitor

In addition to having the components of a computer, you also need a mouse, keyboard, and monitor. Having a mouse and keyboard allows you to control what you are doing on a computer. A mouse allows you to select on what you want to do while a keyboard allows you to implement commands. In order to see what you are doing, the monitor is needed so that you can visually see the commands being executed by the computer.
