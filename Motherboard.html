Motherboard

The motherboard is the main curcuit board for any computer. On the motherboard, there are various parts connected to it like the Hard drive, RAM, CPU, and more. The motherboard connects all parts of a computer and allows them to function with one another for example, information from a USB can transfer information to the hard drive and save its information there. Having a motherboard also is better for your computer rather than having multiple processors that allows the computer to work together. In all, the motherboard allows a computer to connect all of its part together from the monitor to the USB to the HDMI port.
