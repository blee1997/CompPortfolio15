Power Supply

The power supply is the source of energy of a computer. The amount of power your computer requires all depends on how much processing power is needed in addition to the amount of RAM being used. The more energy means that your computer is running harder and sometimes faster. There are occasions where your CPU and RAM are working at their limits causing the computer to slow down and using large amounts of energy.
