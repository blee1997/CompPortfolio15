Hard Drive

A hard disk drive is a data storage device used for storing and retrieving digital information using rapidly rotating disks coated with magnetic material. This works similar to a dvd player where data is stored on a disk. This disk can be altered to where it can either add information or delete information. A hard drive retains its data even when powered off.
